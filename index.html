NAPLES RETREAT

Discover the essence of Naples

There is no city in the world as powerful as Naples. The energy here can be overwhelming, and can be perceived as intrusive, anarchic, loud, disordered… pure creative chaos. But there is another side to Naples: aristocratic, sophisticated, genuine, esoteric, sacred. We will show you this Naples.

We will accompany you on a journey around the city and within yourself to discover physical places as well as places of pure energy. A journey that will transform the way you perceive and understand reality.

Join us for this unique experience

During this interactive retreat, you will visit well-known as well as off-the-beaten-track sites, with guides that will show you and tell you about the esoteric nature of symbols, statues, paintings, that would constitute the hidden language of the initiates.

While on the organised tours we will eat excellent food based on the concept of a balanced meal. This is a meal that contains a variety of nutrients in appropriate proportions to promote overall health and wellbeing. This helps ensure that the meal provides the right amount of carbohydrates, proteins, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals to sustain your daily activities and enhance the experiences we propose during the weekend workshop. We will teach you how to prepare your own balanced meal whenever you want!

The weekend will be fully dedicated to the transformative experiences of the workshop: Exploring Consciousness. This is an intensive 2-day programme that offers the opportunity to explore expanded states of consciousness that facilitate self-discovery, empowerment and personal transformation.

This workshop introduces the participants to the use of the Hemi-Sync® audio technology for the explorations of our consciousness. During the workshop we will practice audio exercises and do engaging activities that provide numerous opportunities for exploration and self-discovery.

The frequencies will enhance and reawaken the natural abilities of your brain and help you realise through direct experience that you are more than your physical body.

Who is this retreat for?

Visits: people who love traveling, but are not tourists. Ideal for those that want to see beyond the surface of things and thrive and are thrilled by knowledge.

Workshop: it is ideal for people who are interested in personal growth, approaching new experiences with an open mind, having a desire to discover their own answers for themselves, new and old.

Overall: those who are not content with passively accepting what they are fed, but rather want to know and make educated choices.
