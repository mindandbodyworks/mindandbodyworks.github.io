TREASURES OF NAPLES

Nourish your Mind, Body and Spirit 10-13 October 2024

Over four days you will discover the powerful creative energy of Naples through guided visits and embark on a journey of self-discovery in a meditation workshop. The energy of Naples can be overwhelming, and can be perceived as intrusive, anarchic, loud, disordered…pure creative chaos. But there is another side to Naples: aristocratic, sophisticated, genuine, esoteric, sacred.

We will show you this Naples. We will accompany you on a journey around the city and within yourself to discover both physical and spiritual treasures.

A journey that will transform the way you perceive and understand reality.

Join us for this unique experience *

We will start the retreat on Thursday afternoon with a warm welcome to all participants: you will meet each other, the facilitators, and will have an introductory activity. You will spend Friday visiting well-known as well as off-the-beaten-track sites, with guides that will show you and tell you about the esoteric nature of symbols, statues, paintings, that still constitutes the hidden language of the initiates.

While on the organised tour we will nourish our bodies with a balanced meal: this is a meal that contains a variety of nutrients in appropriate proportions to promote overall health and wellbeing. This helps ensure that the meal provides the right amount of carbohydrates, proteins, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals to sustain your daily activities and enhance the experiences during the weekend workshop.

Saturday and Sunday will be fully dedicated to a transformative workshop ‘Exploring Consciousness’. This is an intensive 2-day programme that offers the opportunity to explore expanded states of consciousness that facilitate self-discovery, empowerment and personal transformation.

This workshop introduces the participants to the use of the Hemi-Sync® audio technology for the explorations of our consciousness. During the workshop we will listen to guided audio exercises and engage in activities that allow us to experience deep relaxation, expanded consciousness states and the energy body. The workshop will provide numerous opportunities for exploration of other consciousness states and self-discovery.

The Hemi-Sync® sound frequencies will enhance and awaken the natural abilities of your brain and help you realise through direct experience that you are more than your physical body.

Who is this retreat for?

For people who love traveling, but are not tourists. Ideal for those that want to see beyond the surface of things and are thrilled by the esoteric. It is ideal for people who are interested in personal growth, approaching new experiences with an open mind, with a desire to discover their own answers for themselves. Finally, this retreat is for those who are not content with passively accepting what they are fed, but rather, want to know for themselves via direct experience.
