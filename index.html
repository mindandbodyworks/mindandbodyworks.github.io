TREASURES OF NAPLES

Nourish your Mind, Body and Spirit 10-13 October 2024

Over four days you will discover the powerful creative energy of Naples through guided visits and embark on a journey of self-discovery in a meditation workshop. The energy of Naples can be overwhelming, and can be perceived as intrusive, anarchic, loud, disordered…pure creative chaos. But there is another side to Naples: aristocratic, sophisticated, genuine, esoteric, sacred.

We will show you this Naples. We will accompany you on a journey around the city and within yourself to discover both physical and spiritual treasures.

A journey that will transform the way you perceive and understand reality.

Join us for this unique experience *

We will start the retreat on Thursday afternoon with a warm welcome to all participants: you will meet each other, the facilitators, and will have an introductory activity. You will spend Friday visiting well-known as well as off-the-beaten-track sites, with guides that will show you and tell you about the esoteric nature of symbols, statues, paintings, that still constitutes the hidden language of the initiates.

Finally, this retreat is for those who are not content with passively accepting what they are fed, but rather, want to know for themselves via direct experience.

Biographies

Puja MYLES

Puja is a Monroe Institute Outreach Trainer based in the UK and will be co-facilitating the Exploring Consciousness Workshop. Puja has been interested in consciousness exploration and the use of meditation techniques for performance optimization since childhood. She is a Public Health Specialist and Epidemiologist with a longstanding interest in health and wellness. She is also a certified health and wellness coach with extensive experience of teaching in higher education.

Valentina BUCCELLA

Born and raised in Naples, Valentina is a holistic practitioner mustering multiple disciplines and an expert coach. As a certified Monroe Outreach Trainer she delivers in-person workshops in Southern Italy and Switzerland as well as online. Her quest for self-knowledge and wellbeing started over 20 years ago and has urged her to learn and train in multiple disciplines which today form the basis of her unique method and approach to coaching. Val is also a translator and interpreter specialised in esoteric subjects, and an events producer.
